About Auto Parts

Your trusted partner for automotive parts and services since 2005, committed to quality and customer satisfaction.

Our Story

Founded in 2005 by John and Sarah Thompson, Auto Parts began as a small family-owned auto parts store with a mission to provide quality automotive components at fair prices.

What started as a modest operation with just three employees has grown into one of the region's most trusted sources for auto parts and repair services, now serving thousands of satisfied customers each year.

Despite our growth, we remain committed to the personal service and community values that were the foundation of our business.

Our Core Values
